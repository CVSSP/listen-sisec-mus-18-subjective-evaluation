Tutorial

Here you can listen to some audio samples to get yourself familiarised with the types of degradation to be presented during the listening test.

The reference presented here corresponds to the vocals track perfectly extracted from the mix.

The degradation can be in terms of:

  • Distortion in the target (vocals) sound
    • feeling of parts of the target sound removed
    • timbral degradation (e.g., bright/dull)
  • Unwanted sound in addition to the target
    • presence of other tracks from the mix
    • presence of unknown sounds (e.g., noises, “birdies”)

In the test, please grade the stimuli in terms of the overall similarity to the original vocals as the reference.
